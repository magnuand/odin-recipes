Baked salmon

Description

Baked salmon is a common Norwegian dish.

Ingredients

  • Maple syrup
  • Soy saucec
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Make the marinade and cut the salmon into filets.
  2. Marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the fish flakes easily.
